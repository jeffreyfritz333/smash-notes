Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Smash Notes ("the App") is a free, offline note-taking application for tracking fighting game matchup notes. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you create (matchup notes, ratings, and preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage or the app's local data store.

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or any form of user tracking. No cookies are used for tracking purposes.

No Network Requests

The App is designed to work entirely offline. It does not send or receive data over the internet during normal use. The only network activity occurs when initially downloading or updating the App itself.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
